Our Story

A Simple Guide to
Great Coffee

The Grind Guide is a straightforward café directory. We list great independent coffee shops across Ireland, the United Kingdom, France, and the United States — places worth seeking out whether you're a local or passing through.

Every café on this site was chosen for the same reasons: quality coffee, a sense of place, and the kind of atmosphere that makes you want to stay a little longer than you planned. No chains, no sponsored listings, no algorithm. Just hand-picked spots we'd recommend to a friend.

The site started as a rebuild of an old Irish café website — a small place in Ireland that had been closed for years but whose domain still carried some affection. Rather than let it sit idle, we turned it into something useful: a running list of the cafés we think deserve more visitors.

We cover four countries and ten cafés per country, spanning historic Parisian institutions, Edinburgh specialty roasters, Dublin neighbourhood gems, and American independents from Charleston to Seattle. The list grows as we find places worth adding.
